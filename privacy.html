Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025  ·  Effective: March 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Who We Are

NewsTally operates newstally.online and the Socialgati community platform. Contact: newstallyofficial@gmail.com

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your phone number (unless you provide it)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not show targeted ads based on your behavior
  • Reading history stays only on your device (localStorage)

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Platform
  • To show your posts and profile to other users
  • To send you notifications (only if you enable them)
  • To detect and prevent spam or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sheets API — News data. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data practices.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage (browser storage) for:

  • Your dark mode preference
  • News reading history (stays on your device only)
  • Saved articles list
  • Theme color preference

Firebase may use cookies for authentication. You can clear these in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your data
  • Correction — Update your profile information
  • Deletion — Request account deletion (email us)
  • Opt-out — Disable notifications anytime
  • Portability — Request your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, email newstallyofficial@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

NewsTally is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us immediately.

9. Data Security

We use Firebase security rules to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. However, no system is 100% secure. Use a strong password and keep your account credentials safe.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on the Platform. Continued use after changes means acceptance.

11. Contact Us

Privacy questions? Email us at newstallyofficial@gmail.com
